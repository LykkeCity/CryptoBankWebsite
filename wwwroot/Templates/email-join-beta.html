Lykke Modern Wallet is a multisignature wallet, which is like a safe with two keys. You have one key, and Lykke has the other. No one can take your money, even if your phone is stolen. Every transaction is approved by fingerprint and for additional security, we ask for PIN code as well.
The world of finance is changing. Thanks to the distributed ledger technology we can now empower you to take control of your own money and become independent, and still feel secure about your finance. Embrace the change and bring crypto currencies to your everyday life.

From your vault to your wallet

How to retrieve money from the vault?

For additional security, we have placed a time lock when retrieving funds from your Lykke Vault to your personal wallet account. The time lock is a 24 hours time period that prevents the vault from executing a requested transfer of funds until all hours have passed. An email confirmation is also required before starting the time lock period.

On the contrary, you can always transfer money from your Modern Wallet to your Lykke Vault or receive direct external transfers to the Vault when you are in savings mood. Lykke Vault supports the same Portfolio as your standard account, and enables you to save any amount of any currency you want.
